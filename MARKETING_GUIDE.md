# GRYND — Marketing Playbook untuk Instagram & TikTok

> Strategi growth Gen Z. Disesuaikan dengan aplikasi gamified habits Indonesia. Semuanya bisa kamu eksekusi solo tanpa budget besar.

---

## 🎯 Positioning & Brand Voice

**Tagline pilihan (pilih 1 untuk konsisten):**
- "Habit-mu, XP-mu." 🎮
- "Level up IRL."
- "Grinding XP. In real life."
- "Stack streaks. Climb the guild."

**Brand voice:**
- ✅ Lowercase, casual, slang (rizz, sigma, no cap, fr, vibe, slay)
- ✅ Self-aware, meme-y, sarcastic
- ✅ Tidak menggurui (jangan jadi guru BK)
- ❌ Korporat, formal, motivational quotes generik

**Target persona:**
- Mahasiswa & first jobber (18-26)
- Anak Tier-1 / Tier-2 cities Indonesia
- Suka gaming, RPG, Wordle, Duolingo streak
- Productivity-curious tapi anti-cringe

## 📱 STRATEGI TIKTOK (Prioritas #1 — algoritma paling bagus)

TikTok adalah platform terbaik untuk launch app Gen Z di 2026. Reach organic masih besar, konversi ke app install tinggi.

### Format Konten Inti (Buat 5 per minggu, posting 1/hari)

#### 1. **"POV: Streak Hilang" Skit (15-20 detik)**
- Hook: "ketika streak 47 hari hilang karena lupa journaling 1 hari"
- Cut: facial reaction dramatic (look at camera, blank stare)
- Outro: text overlay "GRYND — habit tracker yang kasih XP. di bio."
- **Why this works:** relatable pain point, emosional, gampang di-duet

#### 2. **"Day in the Life — Tapi XP-fied" (30-45 detik)**
- Pagi: tap quest "drink water" → particle burst di app (rekam langsung dari HP)
- Siang: leaderboard close-up — "lagi peringkat 47 global"
- Malam: micro-journal entry 140 chars
- Outro: "level 12 today. masih ngegrind."

#### 3. **"App Demo with Aesthetic Music" (15 detik)**
- Pakai musik trending (cyber/synthwave/lofi)
- Slow-mo swipe-to-complete quest → particle effect
- B-roll: tangan tap HP, layar dengan neon glow
- Hook teks di detik 0-2: "App ini cure habit ku yang berantakan"

#### 4. **"GRYND vs [Competitor]" Comparison**
- Split screen: GRYND di kiri, app boring (Habitica/Streaks) di kanan
- Caption: "habit tracker tapi gak ngegas kalo gak ada XP"
- Emphasize: neon design + Indonesian Saweria support

#### 5. **"Reply to a Comment" Series**
- Cek komentar lama, screenshot, jawab pakai voiceover
- "kenapa harus pakai GRYND padahal udah ada Notion?" → "Notion gak kasih kamu fire streak dan dopamine hit fr"

### Caption Template TikTok

```
[hook 1 baris bold] 
.
[body 2-3 baris santai]
.
gak ada paksaan tapi link di bio
#productivity #habitstacking #genz #gamification 
#aplikasiindo #cybercore #studytok #studywithme
```

### Hashtag Strategy (mix dari 3 tier)

**Tier 1 (high volume, kompetitif):**
`#productivity #studytok #studywithme #habit #selfdevelopment`

**Tier 2 (medium, sweet spot):**
`#habitstacking #productivityapp #gamification #genzproductivity #studymotivation`

**Tier 3 (niche, mudah trending):**
`#aplikasiindo #cyberaesthetic #neonvibes #grindset #questline #leveluplife`

Selalu pakai **2 dari tier 1 + 3 dari tier 2 + 3 dari tier 3** = 8 hashtag total.

### Sound Strategy
- Pakai **trending sounds** (cek di "Discover" tab)
- Atau buat **sound original** — kalau viral, semua orang reuse → free promotion
- Audio Indonesia trending: lagu remix lo-fi, sound effect "BRO?", trending Filipino dubs

### Posting Schedule
- **Best times Indonesia:** 06:00-08:00 (pagi commute), 12:00-13:00 (lunch), 19:00-22:00 (prime)
- **Frequency:** 1 post/hari, minimum 5/minggu
- **Day-of-week:** Senin-Kamis posting baru, Jumat-Minggu repost yang performa bagus

## 📸 STRATEGI INSTAGRAM

### Reels (80% effort)

**Reels = TikTok mirror.** Crosspost konten TikTok ke Reels TAPI:
- ❌ Jangan upload langsung file dengan watermark TikTok (Reels punish ini)
- ✅ Download via Snaptik (clean version) atau re-export tanpa watermark
- ✅ Tambah caption text overlay khusus IG (lebih panjang OK)

**Khusus Reels yang tidak cocok di TikTok:**
- Aesthetic moodboard videos (screen recording app + cinematic music)
- Before/after streak comparisons
- Tutorial cepat 30 detik

### Feed Posts (15% effort)

Carousels 3-5 slides dengan pattern:
- Slide 1: **Hook visual** (screenshot app + 1 kata "VIBES")
- Slide 2-3: **3 features** dengan icon + 1 line caption
- Slide 4: **Social proof** (screenshot review user)
- Slide 5: **CTA** ("link di bio · gratis")

Pakai template Canva / Figma dengan **palet GRYND** (#000, #00E5FF, #39FF14, #B026FF).

### Stories (5% effort, daily)

Daily story format:
1. **Pagi:** "morning quest log" — screenshot Hub kamu sendiri
2. **Siang:** "leaderboard check" — share progress
3. **Malam:** "journal entry of the day" — quote dari user (consent dulu)

Gunakan stickers:
- ❤️ Poll: "udah ngegrind hari ini?"
- 📊 Quiz: "berapa hari streak terlama kamu?"
- 🔗 Link sticker ke website / Play Store

### Instagram Bio Template

```
GRYND
habit-mu, XP-mu. 🎮
gamified self-development for Gen Z 🇮🇩
↓ download grynd ↓
[link.tree/grynd]
```

Bio link → Linktree dengan:
- Download Android APK
- Download di Play Store (after launch)
- Saweria (support devs)
- Instagram → "@grynd.app"
- TikTok → "@grynd.app"

## 🚀 PRE-LAUNCH (2 minggu sebelum app rilis)

### Week -2: Tease

**Day 1-3: Drop teaser**
- TikTok/Reels 15-detik dengan tulisan "something cyber dropping soon..."
- Show 3-second glimpses of app (blur dulu)
- Caption: "habit tracker buat anak Gen Z yang anti-cringe. soon."

**Day 4-7: Build email/waitlist**
- Setup Tally.so / Google Form gratis untuk waitlist
- Insentif: "join waitlist = dapat Guild Pro gratis selamanya untuk 100 pertama"
- Drop di TikTok "join waitlist link di bio. cuma 100 spot"

### Week -1: Hype

**Day 8-12: Feature drops**
- 1 video per feature: Quests, Journal, Leaderboard, Cosmetics, Streak
- Each video 15-20 sec, fast cuts, app screen recording
- CTA: "link di bio buat join waitlist"

**Day 13-14: Countdown**
- Story countdown sticker
- Pin a post / video "T-2 days, T-1 day, IT'S OUT"

## 💰 PAID ADS (Hanya kalau organic udah jalan)

**Jangan langsung paid ads.** Validate organic dulu (min 1000 organic installs), baru scale.

### TikTok Ads
- **Budget awal:** IDR 100K-500K/hari
- **Tipe:** Spark Ads (boost video organic yang udah perform)
- **Target:** Indonesia, 18-26, interest: productivity/gaming/anime/aesthetic
- **CTA:** Download App / Visit Website

### Instagram/Meta Ads
- **Budget awal:** IDR 50K-300K/hari
- **Tipe:** Reels Ads (looks native)
- **Tujuan:** App Install (kalau udah di Play Store)
- **Custom Audience:** Lookalike dari user yang sign-up

## 🔁 ENGAGEMENT LOOPS (yang harus dibangun in-app)

Marketing eksternal aja gak cukup. Buat app-mu jadi shareable:

1. **Share Streak Screenshot** — tombol di Hub: "share my streak" → generate cool image (background neon + level + days) → user post organic
2. **Leaderboard Brag** — kalau user masuk top 100, push notification + tombol "share rank"
3. **Daily Story Template** — di profile, tombol "share to IG story" dengan template GRYND-branded
4. **Referral Reward** — "invite friend → both get 1 week 2× XP boost"
5. **Pro Mode Cosmetic Showoff** — Pro users dapat watermarked screenshot lebih cantik

## ⚠️ THINGS TO AVOID

- ❌ Posting tanpa hook di 3 detik pertama (auto-skip)
- ❌ Voice yang terlalu polished / corporate
- ❌ Quote motivasi tanpa visual (cringe)
- ❌ Spam DM tanpa context
- ❌ Beli followers (algoritma TikTok benci akun fake-engagement)
- ❌ Comparison ke competitor dengan tone benci (jangan ngebash)
- ❌ Drop link tanpa context — selalu add value dulu, baru CTA
